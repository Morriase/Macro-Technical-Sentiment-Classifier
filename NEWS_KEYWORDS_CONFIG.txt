How to Use in EA:
-----------------
- For each pair, input the combined keywords into the "keyNews" parameter.
- Separate keywords with commas or semicolons as per the "separator" setting.
- The EA will monitor news events containing these keywords for the relevant currencies.
- Adjust "DaysNewsLookup" and "StopBeforeMin" to control how far ahead and how long before news to avoid trading.

Note: These keywords are derived from common economic indicators and central bank references. Customize as needed based on your trading strategy.
